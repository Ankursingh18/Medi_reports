Diseases & Remedies :



Malaria

    Remedies :
  • Rest and avoid strenuous activities.
  • Drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated.
  • Take prescribed antibiotics as directed.
  • Eat a simple, nutritious diet.
  • Use cool baths or cloths to reduce fever.

Typhoid

    Remedies :
  • Apply mosquito repellent with DEET (diethyltoluamide) to exposed skin.
  • Drape mosquito netting over beds.
  • Put screens on windows and doors.
  • Treat clothing, mosquito nets, tents, sleeping bags and other fabrics with an insect repellent called permethrin.
  • Wear long pants and long sleeves to cover your skin.

Cancer

    Remedies :
  • Follow doctor's treatment plan, which may include surgery, chemotherapy, or other therapies.
  • Eat a healthy diet with fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
  • Stay active with gentle exercises if possible.
  • Get support from loved ones or cancer support groups.
  • Manage symptoms like pain, nausea, and fatigue with medications or therapies.

food poisining

    Remedies :
  • Drink plenty of water to prevent dehydration caused by vomiting and diarrhea.
  • Eat bland, easy-to-digest foods like bananas, rice, applesauce, and toast to settle the stomach.
  • Stay away from spicy, fatty, or dairy-rich foods that may aggravate symptoms.
  • Get plenty of rest to help your body recover.
  • Consider drinking oral rehydration solutions or sports drinks to replenish lost electrolytes.

diabities

    Remedies :
  • Eat balanced meals with fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
  • Stay active with regular exercise.
  • Take prescribed medications as directed.
  • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly.
  • Maintain a healthy weight.

headache

    Remedies :
  • Stay hydrated by drinking water.
  • Take a break from screens and bright lights.
  • Apply a cold compress to the forehead.
  • Ensure you're getting enough sleep.
  • Massage tense muscles in the neck and shoulders.
